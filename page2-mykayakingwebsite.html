Welcome to the sport!

Kayaking is an amazing sport and an excellent way to get outside. If you are interested in getting into kayaking, you should look into buying the following safety gear:

  • Helmet
  • Personal flotation device (PFD)
  • Throw bag
  • River knife
  • Water filtration
  • Sunblock
  • Technical outerwear

Choosing a Boat
